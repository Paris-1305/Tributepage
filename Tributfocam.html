PAUL KENMOGNE FOKAM

Biography

Paul Fokam,born on july 27,1948 in Mboukue in the city of Baham(Cameroon), is a Cameroonian banker,investor and entrepreneur and one of the largest fortunes in French-speaking Africa.

Beginnings and formation

Paul Fokam is a graduate in economic management from the CNAM in Paris and holds a doctorate in management sciences from the University of Bordeaux in 1989

Career

In 1986,Paul Fokam founded CCEI Bank, a Cameroonian bank which became Afriland First Bank in 2002.since 2008,he has been chairman of the board of directors of the Afriland First Group holding company based in Switzerland.A teacher of leadership and strategy,he founded PKFokam Institute of Excellence in 2006.Paul Fokam is the designer and creator of the MC2 model,the first unit of which was launched in 1992 in Cameroon In 2006, he created Voxafrica, a pan-African television channel based in London.Paul Fokam has been in the ranking of the wealthiest in French-speaking Sub-Saharan Africa since 2015.In 2020,he was appointed chairman of the Board of Directors of the Banque National d'investissement de Guinee(BNIG).
